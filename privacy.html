H2U Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to H2U ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal data when you use our AI companion and social application (the "App").

We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the Singapore Personal Data Protection Act 2012 ("PDPA") and other applicable data protection laws.

By using H2U, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal data as described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Username/display name
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Date of birth (for age verification)
  • Profile information you choose to provide

2.2 Photos and Images

When you upload photos to create your anime-style avatar, we process these images using AI technology. Your original photos are processed temporarily and are not stored permanently on our servers after avatar generation is complete.

2.3 Chat and Conversation Data

We collect and store your conversations with AI companions to:

  • Provide personalized AI responses
  • Maintain conversation history and context ("memory")
  • Improve the quality of AI interactions
  • Enable features you have requested

Your chat data is processed by AI services to generate responses. We do not use your private conversations for advertising purposes.

2.4 Voice Data

If you use voice features, we process your voice input to convert speech to text and generate AI voice responses. Voice data is processed in real-time and is not permanently stored.

2.5 Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • App usage statistics
  • IP address and general location (country/region)
  • Crash reports and performance data

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's features and services
  • Create and manage your AI companion avatars
  • Process and respond to your conversations with AI companions
  • Personalize your experience based on your preferences
  • Verify your identity and age
  • Process payments for premium features
  • Communicate with you about updates and support
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Third-Party Services and Data Transfers

To provide our services, we share data with the following third-party service providers:

4.1 AI Processing Services

Google (Gemini API): Processes your text conversations to generate AI responses. Your messages are sent to Google's servers in the United States for processing.

4.2 Voice Services

Fish Audio: Provides text-to-speech services for AI voice responses.

4.3 Image Processing

We use AI image generation services to convert your photos into anime-style avatars. Your photos are transmitted to these services for processing and are deleted after avatar generation.

4.4 Infrastructure Services

  • Supabase: Provides database and authentication services
  • Apple App Store: Processes payments for subscriptions
Cross-Border Data Transfers: Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside Singapore, including the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with the PDPA.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • Conversation history is retained to maintain AI memory features
  • Photos uploaded for avatar creation are deleted after processing
  • When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days

6. Your Rights Under PDPA

Under the Singapore PDPA, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data held by us
  • Correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Request information about how your data has been used

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls limiting who can access your data

8. Children's Privacy

H2U is not intended for users under 17 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 17. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 17, we will delete that information promptly.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date.
